Refund & Cancellation Policy

In-App Purchases & Event Fees: DormDeal processes payments through Stripe for event creation fees and donations. Due to the immediate nature of digital transactions:

  • Event creation fees are non-refundable once an event is posted
  • Donations are final and non-refundable
  • For billing disputes or technical issues, contact us at getrebookedapp@gmail.com

Marketplace Transactions: DormDeal facilitates connections between students for in-person transactions. We do not process payments for marketplace items.

  • All marketplace transactions are conducted directly between students
  • DormDeal is not responsible for disputes between buyers and sellers
  • We recommend meeting in safe, public campus locations
  • Report any suspicious activity to @dormdealofficial

Cancellations: You may delete your account at any time through the app settings. Account deletion is permanent and cannot be undone.

Terms of Service

Eligibility: DormDeal is exclusively for currently enrolled college and university students. Users must verify their student status with a valid .edu email address.

User Conduct: Users agree to:

  • Provide accurate information in listings
  • Conduct transactions honestly and in good faith
  • Meet in safe, public campus locations for exchanges
  • Not engage in fraudulent, illegal, or harmful activity
  • Respect other users and campus community guidelines

Platform Use: DormDeal is a platform connecting students for local, in-person transactions. We do not:

  • Process payments for marketplace items
  • Ship or deliver items
  • Guarantee the quality, safety, or legality of listed items
  • Assume liability for transactions between users

Prohibited Items: Users may not list weapons, illegal substances, alcohol, tobacco, stolen goods, or any items prohibited by campus policies or law.

Account Termination: DormDeal reserves the right to suspend or terminate accounts for violations of these terms, fraudulent activity, or other conduct harmful to the community.
