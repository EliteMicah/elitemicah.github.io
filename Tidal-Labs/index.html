How It Works

Simple Setup, Automatic Clips

No camera operator needed. You surf, Tidal Labs handles the rest.

📱

Set Up Your iPhone

Position your iPhone on the beach pointing at the break. Hit record in the app.

⌚

Wear Your Watch

Strap on your Apple Watch. Scroll the crown to start and stop logging a timestamp for each wave you catch.

🔄

Sync After Your Session

Open the app on your iPhone. Watch data syncs wirelessly in seconds.

✂️

Get Your Clips

The app cross-references timestamps with the footage and trims each wave automatically.

Features

Everything a Surfer Needs

Designed for solo surfers who want their sessions captured without a film crew.

⌚

Watch-Powered Timestamps

Scroll to start your session, scroll to end it. Your Apple Watch logs a precise timestamp for each wave you catch in between.

✂️

Automatic Clip Trimming

The app matches Watch timestamps to your iPhone footage and trims each ride into its own clip automatically.

📂

Session Library

All your sessions and clips are organized by date and location. Review your progression over time.

🔒

Private by Default

Your footage and session data stay on your device. We never upload your clips to any server.

🎞️

Share Your Best Waves

Export individual clips to your Camera Roll and share anywhere with one tap.

🏄

Solo Surfer Friendly

No buddies, no tripods with operators. Just you, your gear, and a fixed iPhone angle.

Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 2026

Tidal Labs ("the App") is developed and operated by Micah Woodring. This Privacy Policy explains what data we collect, how we use it, and how we protect it.

1. Data We Collect

Tidal Labs is designed to process all data locally on your device. Specifically, we may collect or access:

  • Camera access — to record video through your iPhone camera during sessions
  • Apple Watch motion and sensor data — to detect and timestamp wave events during your surf session
  • Local storage — video clips and session data are stored on your device only
  • App usage data — anonymous crash logs and diagnostics may be collected through Apple's standard crash reporting, subject to your system-level privacy settings

2. Data We Do Not Collect

  • We do not collect, upload, or store your video footage on any external server
  • We do not collect your name, email address, or any account information
  • We do not share any data with third parties for advertising or analytics
  • We do not use location data beyond what you optionally provide for session labeling

3. Apple Watch Data

Sensor and motion data from your Apple Watch is used exclusively to detect wave timestamps during an active recording session. This data is processed on-device and is not transmitted anywhere.

4. Camera & Microphone

The App requires camera access to record footage. Microphone access may be requested as part of standard video recording. Recorded footage is saved directly to your device and is never uploaded by the App.

5. Third-Party Services

Tidal Labs does not integrate with any third-party analytics, advertising, or data broker services.

6. Children's Privacy

The App is not directed to children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Continued use of the App after any changes constitutes acceptance of the new policy. The "last updated" date at the top reflects the most recent revision.
